Juggernaut

Yurnero, The Juggernaut, is a melee agility hero whose abilities allow him to sprint into battle and recklessly devastate enemies in an impenetrable flurry of blades.

Phantom Assasin

Mortred, The Phantom Assasin, is a melee agility hero fitting the role of hard carry. Mortred is best-known, and infamous for, her ability to inflict staggerring damage with single strikes.

Shadowfiend

Nevermore, The Shadowfiend, is a ranged agility hero possessing abilities that inflict super burst damage from varying distances. whether near or far, Shadowfiend is able to release incredible offensive power, both physical and magical.
